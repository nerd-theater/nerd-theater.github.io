Last On The Bandwagon

January 18, 2012   

If every fan of Nerd Theater banded together, we might be able to afford a cup of coffee!

Dancinpete: Would it be instant or drip coffee?

Mike: Trash coffee. We would scrounge through refuse in order to recover sufficient quantities of richer folks' coffee-laden spittle.

Dancinpete: I must know... In the last frame...is that turd under your toupee?

Mike: It's supposed to be blood, but now that you mention it...

Dancinpete: Oh. ... Oh! You...you're doin' good.

Mike: Fixed it. Who says great art can't change?

Dancinpete: I hope the turd under your ego isn't too bruised!

Anonymous Fungus: Panel One: The hero of the comic breaks (?) a bar of soap. This is the source of humor.

Anonymous Fungus: Panel Two: Mike (with the M shirt) speaks to Alex (with the A shirt) and says "you poor ignorant fool" which is funny.

Anonymous Fungus: Panel Three: Violence.

Mike: Astute observations there, Fungus! If you join our Nerd Theater Summer Internship program, Alex promises to only molest you sporadically (when not intoxicated).

Dancinpete: I hope Fungus continues to explain all of your comics panel by panel. It's so enriching.
